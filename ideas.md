# JJCleanRides — Design Brainstorm

## Brief
Single-page scrollable site for a mobile car valeting business. Primary: Orbit Navy. Accent: Electric Blue. Premium, luxury detailing studio feel. Mobile-first (Instagram traffic).

---

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Dark Luxury Automotive — inspired by high-end car showrooms and premium detailing studios (think Gtechniq, Gyeon, Ceramic Pro)

**Core Principles:**
1. Deep navy backgrounds with electric blue accents create a premium night-garage atmosphere
2. Asymmetric section layouts break the monotony of standard landing pages
3. Generous whitespace with tight typographic hierarchy signals confidence
4. Every interactive element has a purposeful glow or shimmer effect

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Deep Orbit Navy (#0B1220) — the colour of a dark showroom floor
- Primary accent: Electric Blue (#0EA5E9) — like LED underglow or detailing lights
- Secondary: Cool white (#F0F4FF) for text — crisp against dark backgrounds
- Subtle: Slate grey (#1E2D40) for card surfaces — layered depth

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: Full-bleed image with a diagonal cut at the bottom, text anchored bottom-left
- Services: Two cards with a thin electric-blue vertical divider between them, offset vertically
- How It Works: Horizontal numbered flow with connecting dotted lines
- Gallery: Masonry-style grid with hover zoom + lightbox
- About: Split panel — photo left, text right with a large decorative quote mark

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin electric-blue horizontal rule lines as section dividers
2. Subtle grain texture overlay on dark backgrounds for depth
3. Glowing CTA buttons with a blue halo on hover

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Smooth, deliberate. Sections fade in on scroll. Buttons have a ripple/glow effect. Gallery images scale on hover. Navigation sticks at top with a frosted glass effect.

**Animation:**
- Scroll-triggered fade-up for section content (staggered children)
- Hero text slides in from left on load
- Button hover: blue glow expands outward
- Gallery: scale(1.05) + brightness boost on hover

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Bebas Neue" — bold, condensed, automotive energy for headings
- Body: "DM Sans" — clean, modern, highly readable
- Accent labels: "DM Mono" — for prices, step numbers, small caps labels

## Chosen Approach: Dark Luxury Automotive (Response 1)

**Rationale:** Best matches the brief — premium detailing studio feel, Orbit Navy + Electric Blue palette, bold headings, mobile-first. The asymmetric layouts and glow effects will make this stand out from generic local business sites.
